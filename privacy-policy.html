Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Michael Christopher Provenzano (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use our mobile application F.D.A. — Food Decision App (the “App”), available on iOS and Android.

We built F.D.A. to help people decide what to eat. We keep data on your device. We do not run servers that receive your personal information, and we do not sell your data.

Summary

  • No account required — the App works without sign-in today.
  • On-device only — your cards, choices, and images stay on your phone or tablet.
  • Limited access — the App may ask to access photos or files on your device so you can add pictures to cards. Those images are not uploaded to us.
  • No ads, no analytics, no third-party trackers — as of the effective date above.
  • Free to use — optional one-time in-app support purchases may be offered in a future update.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information on our own servers because we do not operate a backend that receives your data.

The App may store the following locally on your device:

  • Food cards, preferences, and other content you create in the App
  • Images you choose from your device’s photo library or file storage to display on cards
  • App settings and preferences

We do not currently collect:

  • Name, email, or account credentials (no login is required today)
  • Location, contacts, microphone, or calendar data
  • Analytics, advertising identifiers, or crash reports sent to third parties

Device permissions

The App may request access to photos or files on your device so you can attach images to cards. You can deny permission; some features may not work without it.

Images and other content you select remain on your device. They are not transmitted to us or to third-party services.

How we use information

Information stored on your device is used only to:

  • Run the App and show your cards and decisions
  • Remember your in-app preferences
  • Let you add images you choose from your device

We do not use your information for advertising or profiling.

Accounts and future features

The App does not require an account today. We may add optional sign-in (for example, email or Apple/Google sign-in) in a future version. If we do, we will update this Privacy Policy before or when that feature launches and explain what new data is collected and why.

We may also offer an optional one-time in-app purchase so users can show support. Payment processing is handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not receive your full payment card details. Purchase history is managed by the store provider under their privacy policies.

Third-party services

As of the effective date above, the App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or cloud backends.

If you make an in-app purchase in a future version, Apple or Google will process the transaction. Please review their privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Data storage and retention

All App data is stored on your device only. We do not copy it to our servers.

Data remains on your device until you delete it in the App, clear the App’s storage in your device settings, or uninstall the App.

Sharing of information

We do not share your personal information with other users, social networks, or advertisers through the App. The App has no chat, public profiles, or social features.

You may choose to share screenshots or other content from the App to social media or messaging apps outside the App. That sharing is under your control and is not initiated by us.

We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Children’s privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

International users

The App is available globally. Michael Christopher Provenzano is based in Vietnam. Because data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers, cross-border transfer by us is limited. Device and store providers (Apple/Google) may process data according to their own policies.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data.

  • Delete in the App: use in-app controls to remove your data.
  • Delete on your device: uninstall the App or clear its data in iOS/Android settings.
  • Contact us: email mike_proven@outlook.com with privacy questions.

Residents of the EEA, UK, and California may have additional rights under applicable law. Contact us to exercise them.

Security

We use reasonable measures in how we design the App. Data on your device is protected by your device’s operating system and any lock screen or encryption you use. No method of storage is 100% secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when we add features (such as accounts or in-app purchases). We will post the updated version on this page and change the effective date. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
