# Q2 2024 – Finance content push (internal notes)

**Campaign:** CAMP-Q1-PUSH (extended into April)

- Boosted investing basics and retirement planning pieces in Jan–Feb. CTR was strong (above 2.5%) but conversions to subscription were lower than we wanted.
- In March we shifted more budget to the newsletter signup campaign (CAMP-Q1-SUB). Sub growth was better; cost per sub still high (~$22).
- Content that performed best: CONT-003 (retirement piece), CONT-001 (budgeting). CONT-004 (FinTech) had good impressions but lower engagement.
- Next: test more affiliate links in the budgeting/saving articles and track revenue per post. Need to reconcile March spend with finance – some rows missing in export.
